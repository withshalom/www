About Michael
"Music with style and meaning." Michael Sean Miller

Michael Sean Miller is a Sacramento-based professional guitarist, wedding host, and DJ entertainer with a lifelong passion for creating meaningful musical experiences at live events.

With a mastery of classical and Spanish guitar, Michael has performed at some of Northern California's finest restaurants, wineries, ranches, and wedding venues — bringing technical artistry and genuine warmth to every show.

As a wedding host and MC, Michael combines the elegance of live guitar with high-energy entertainment, guiding each couple's special day from ceremony to last dance with professionalism and heart.

Whether you're booking a romantic dinner residency or a full-day wedding experience, Michael delivers a performance that feels personal — because it is.
